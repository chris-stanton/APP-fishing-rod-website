Employment Opportunites

CS is always looking for ice rod wrappers. How it works: CS Rod Wrappers work remotely from thier own workshops. The wrappars come into the store and pick up ice rod blacks with handles pre-glued onto them. The Wrapper is responsable for wrapping the guides and hook keepers onto each ice rod. CS will provide all the hardware and supplies neccessary to complete the job. Supplies include: guides, hook keepers, thread, razor blades and tape. Each wrapping is required to have there own wrapping jig and thread tensioners. CS builds ice rods year round to keep up with the demand.
